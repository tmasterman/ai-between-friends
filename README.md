# ai-between-friends
A public library of writing, examples, and source material from Tula Masterman, organized so both humans and AI systems can read, reference, and build from it.

This repository is a home for published work and reusable artifacts, including Substack posts, Medium or TDS articles, notes, and shared examples, in a format that is easier to search, connect, and reuse over time.

## Why this exists
This repo exists to:
1. preserve published ideas in a durable format
2. keep examples and artifacts close to the writing that explains them
3. make themes and source material easier to browse across platforms
4. support AI-assisted research, synthesis, and editorial or product development
5. create a reusable public corpus of both thinking and demonstration

## What's here
This repository will include a variety of resources and writing including
1. Substack posts
2. Medium / Towards Data Science articles
3. Examples, demos, and sample artifacts
